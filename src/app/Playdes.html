About The Event


One of the longest running shows at London’s West End, MAMMA MIA! tells a tale of love and friendship, set on an idyllic Greek island where the sun always shines. This heart-warming story of a mother and daughter unfolds to the timeless hits of legendary Swedish band ABBA, including ‘Dancing Queen’, ‘Super Trouper’, ‘Honey, Honey’, ‘Voulez-Vous’, ‘Gimme! Gimme! Gimme!’ and many more. Sophie’s quest to discover the father she’s never known brings her mother face to face with three men from her distant romantic past on the eve of a wedding they’ll never forget! Come celebrate the power of love and music with this wholesome family entertainer at The Grand Theatre at Nita Mukesh Ambani Cultural Centre. With music & lyrics by Benny Andersson & Björn Ulvaeus, MAMMA MIA! is written by Catherine Johnson, directed by Phyllida Lloyd and choreographed by Anthony Van Laast. The production is designed by Mark Thompson, with lighting design by Howard Harrison, sound design by Andrew Bruce & Bobby Aitken, and musical supervision, additional material & arrangements by Martin Koch.
